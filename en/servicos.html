What we offer

Services

We cover the entire lifecycle of access infrastructure — from construction to maintenance — for operators, private companies and public entities.

01

Survey & design

Technical field survey and project development, with integrated management and coordination — the foundation for rigorous delivery.

  • Technical survey
  • Field assessment
  • Project development
  • Project management & coordination
02

Network construction

Construction of access infrastructure across aerial and underground networks, from route to connection, coordinated with the relevant authorities.

  • Aerial networks
  • Underground networks
  • Ducts
  • Routes
  • Poles & supports
03

Optical fibre & copper

Delivery, maintenance and modernisation of optical fibre (FTTH) and copper infrastructure, from the main span to the access point.

  • Fibre laying
  • Splicing
  • Copper cables
  • FTTH
  • Connections
  • Access point
04

Installation & service activation

Installation of equipment and technical solutions, activation and delivery of telecommunications services to the end customer, end-to-end.

  • Equipment installation
  • Service activation
  • End-customer delivery
  • End-to-end (E2E)
05

Supervision & monitoring

Continuous supervision and monitoring of networks, ensuring operational control, performance and alignment of field operations.

  • Network supervision
  • Monitoring
  • Operational control
  • Operations management
06

Maintenance & repair

Preventive and corrective maintenance, with fault diagnosis, detection and repair and fast response to reduce downtime.

  • Preventive maintenance
  • Corrective maintenance
  • Fault diagnosis
  • Repair
  • Fast response (SLA)
07

Expansion & optimisation

Extension, modernisation and optimisation of existing networks, keeping pace with demand and technological evolution.

  • Route extension
  • Modernisation
  • Network optimisation
  • Densification
08

Quality & auditing

Quality control and technical auditing of networks and infrastructure, with verification and certification of the work delivered.

  • Quality control
  • Network auditing
  • Certification
  • Technical verification
09

Warehousing & logistics

Warehousing and inventory management, logistics management and technical support — ensuring availability, traceability and operational continuity.

  • Warehouse management
  • Material receipt & control
  • Traceability
  • Logistics management
  • Technical support
