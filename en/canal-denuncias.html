Internal Whistleblowing Channel

Império Global provides this channel to receive, under conditions of confidentiality and protection of the whistleblower, reports of irregularities or infringements witnessed in a professional context. This channel complies with Portuguese Law 93/2021 of 20 December, which transposes Directive (EU) 2019/1937 of the European Parliament and of the Council on the protection of persons who report breaches of Union law.

What you can report

  • Public procurement
  • Financial services, products and markets and prevention of money laundering
  • Product safety and compliance
  • Transport safety
  • Protection of the environment
  • Food safety and animal health
  • Public health
  • Consumer protection
  • Protection of privacy and personal data, and security of networks and information systems
  • Corruption, fraud and conflicts of interest
  • Harassment and discrimination in a professional context

Guarantees

  • Confidentiality of the whistleblower and of third parties mentioned in the report
  • Possibility of submitting an anonymous report
  • Protection against retaliation, under Article 21 of Law 93/2021
  • Reports handled by an internally designated officer, impartially
  • Acknowledgement of receipt within 7 days and follow-up within a maximum of 3 months

How to report

Two channels are available in parallel. To preserve anonymity, we recommend the form, leaving the email field blank.

Form (recommended for anonymity)

Complete the fields below. The email field is optional — to remain anonymous, leave it blank.
